TASK1 Professional Writing Characteristics

Evaluating 'Simple Recipe's' Website

Characteristic Description
Active Voice The website often uses active voice and second-person pronouns that directly engage the reader, making the instructions feel personal and easy to follow.
Strong Nouns and Verbs Effectively uses strong nouns and verbs, benefiting recipes and instructions with precise, action-oriented language that helps readers visualize the process and outcomes.
Biases Simple Recipes may unintentionally reflect biases, such as prioritizing Western-style cooking techniques and ingredients, potentially overlooking global cuisines or alternative cooking traditions.
Parallelism Evidence of parallelism, especially in step-by-step directions, to maintain clarity and consistency (e.g., "Save time, reduce waste, and enjoy delicious meals with our recipes").
Clarity Prioritizes clarity by using concise sentences and straightforward instructions.
Sentence Variety Demonstrates effective sentence variety, mixing short, direct instructions with more detailed explanations to prevent monotony.
Conciseness Excels in conciseness, delivering clear instructions without unnecessary details, making it easy for users to follow recipes.
Tone and Voice The tone is approachable and friendly, aiming to engage readers while maintaining professionalism.
Conversational Prose Uses conversational language, ensuring content flows smoothly and avoids technical jargon.
Simple Words Simple, everyday language is used, ensuring accessibility for a wide audience.
Correctness Adheres to editorial guidelines to ensure content accuracy.
Shorter Paragraphs Content is broken into short, digestible paragraphs, enhancing readability.

TASK2:The AWE Writing Process

Assess: We aim to empower University of Glasgow students with easy-to-follow recipes that reduce food waste and promote sustainability.

  • Purpose: Simplify cooking with available ingredients.
  • Audience: Primarily University of Glasgow students, then expanding to others.
  • Content Needs: Clear instructions, sustainability tips, and interactive features like an ingredient search bar.

Write: The content is written in a friendly, motivational tone to encourage students. From recipes to sustainability tips, everything is designed to be simple and accessible.

Edit: We ensure consistency across pages, correct any grammatical errors, and refine the user experience based on feedback to keep the platform engaging and functional.

Reflection on the Analysis

At first, I based my analysis of The Student Pantry's writing on my own judgment. However, after using Voyant Tool for some data-driven analysis, I got a clearer picture of how vocabulary is used and what words pop up most. Other food sustainability and student websites helped me see some common trends. Now, I’m using that info to tweak The Student Pantry’s content and ensure the language is more engaging and effective for the audience.
