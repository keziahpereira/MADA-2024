Research

The research for The Student Pantry focused on understanding the challenges students face with food preparation and waste. I looked into food sustainability practices, common pantry staples, and simple recipes that can be made with minimal ingredients. I also explored existing platforms like BBC Good Food and Allrecipes to see how they organize recipes and cater to their audiences. Additionally, I gathered insights from fellow students at the University of Glasgow to ensure the website meets their needs, particularly regarding ease of use and accessibility. The goal was to create a resource that genuinely helps students save time, money, and reduce food waste.

Task 2: Topic Introduction

The current issue of food waste and limited access to affordable ingredients has caused significant challenges for students. Many struggle with planning meals or utilizing their groceries effectively, leading to food waste and poor nutrition.

Examples of challenges include:

  • Inconsistent access to fresh ingredients
  • Limited time for meal preparation
  • Inability to use all groceries before they spoil

To address these issues, I propose a website called The Student Pantry. This website is designed to assist users in making the most of the ingredients in their pantry. It will assume that users have salt and water.

The main functions of the website include:

  1. Users must enter their ingredients in the search bar.
  2. Once entered, the website will provide links to easy, accessible recipes.
  3. The website will allow users to choose the difficulty level of the recipe.
  4. There will also be a waiting game for users to play while their meals are cooking.

Task 3: Target Audience

Target Audience

The main target audience for The Student Pantry is students at the University of Glasgow. This platform will later be marketed to students at other universities.

Benefits of the Website

  • Empowering users to create meals without additional purchases.
  • Encouraging sustainability by minimizing food waste.
  • Increasing awareness of food waste and food scarcity issues.
  • Maximizing the use of available ingredients.

Engagement with Users

The website will incorporate features for user engagement, including:

  • A feedback section where users can share their thoughts on recipes.
  • Option for users to add their own recipes.
  • Discussion forums where users can share cooking tips and sustainability practices.
