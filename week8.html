Task 1: Audio Analysis

Website: BBC Good Food

Purpose of Audio: Speech/Instructional voiceovers in recipe videos

Intent: Guides users through recipes step-by-step. The voiceover aligns with visual cues, enhancing comprehension and helping users multitask (e.g., listening while cooking).

Advantages: Helps users follow instructions more effectively than just text.

Caveats: Requires clear, high-quality recordings and overuse of voiceovers without an option to mute might frustrate users.

Integration with media: Voiceovers complement visuals (e.g., close-ups of cooking steps), enhancing retention and appeal.

Website: Tasty.co

Purpose of Audio: Sound effects (e.g., chopping, sizzling) and background music.

Intent: Creates an immersive experience by simulating the cooking environment.

Advantages: Provides an engaging and sensory-rich experience and reinforces visual cues, such as the sound of oil sizzling when it’s ready for frying.

Caveats: Background music can distract users focusing on instructions.

Integration with media: The music complements the theme but could benefit from user controls like a mute button.

Website: AllRecipes

Purpose of Audio: Background music and ambient sounds in tutorial videos.

Intent: Adds an element of relaxation and engagement, making cooking feel less intimidating.

Advantages: Enhances the mood and encourages longer user engagement.

Caveats: Audio needs to match the tone of the website and user expectations.

Integration with media: Music complements text instructions and slow-paced videos for a calming effect.

Website: Awwwards

Purpose of Audio: Ambient sounds and interactive sound effects.

Intent: Creates immersive experiences and encourages exploration of the website.

Advantages: Improves the storytelling aspect, especially for narrative-based websites.

Caveats: Overuse can lead to sensory overload.

Integration with media: Seamless combination of sound with animations and videos to tell a cohesive story.

Analysis: I chose jazz music for The Student Pantry because it creates a relaxed and welcoming vibe that fits perfectly with the goal of helping students with meal prep.
